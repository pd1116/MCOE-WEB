ABOUT APP CLUB

Department of Information Technology of Modern College of Engineering Pune-05 unveiled APP CLUB under Information Technology Student Association (ITSA) from year 2015. This APP Club was inaugurated on Engineerâ€™s Day Wednesday, 15/09/2015 by Mr. Rahul Gurjar (working as a Sr. Program Manager at TCL Pvt.Ltd) in the presence of HOD Prof. (Mrs.) S.D.Deshpande, ITSA Coordinator Mrs. Bhawana Chaudhary and all faculty of IT department in I.T. Seminar hall.

This club consists of faculties and students who truly care about the creation of new ideas through the use of software development. We facilitate this by teaching each other through workshops and training. We're an open source community, and to us, that means that we accept everyone willing to learn or give back through teaching.

Vision

To develop proficient IT engineers for the Industry and Society.

Mission

  • To develop students for being competent in dynamic IT environment.
  • To achieve academic excellence.
  • To encourage research and innovation.
  • To inculcate moral and professional ethics
